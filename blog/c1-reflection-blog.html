What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

I feel that EDA is going to be place that I can learn programming skills while also learning how to work well with others. This is something that I am excited about because I believe you need to have both skill sets.

What are your impressions?

I am really excited to be part of EDA! I feel like I am going to be pushed outside of my comfort zone and I will be learning a lot of news concepts, but I also feel I will be able to do this because of the supportive environment.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I think that this type of culture of working with others will be something that I will enjoy being part of. At times it can take me awhile to really open up to others, however, once I get to know people I find working alongside and learning from others really rewarding. It is great that there are weekly ‘phase-0 group check ins’ where I am able to meet others and start forming relationships before boot camp starts.

Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

I don't think my expectations of EDA have changed.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

I am very excited to be participating in this kind of learning environment as I like working with and learning from others. I am also nervous, as I have the first fear Shereef mentioned in his video of "I don't know that much"! However, when watching the video I was reassured that each person has a ton to offer and a ton to learn and there are other people in the same position as me.
