Self Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness and self awareness are very similar. Mindfulness is being conscious or aware of something in the present moment and self awareness is being conscious or aware of your own feelings.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I tried the body scan. During the body scan I felt relaxed and I can definitely see why people would end up falling asleep! However, when it got to the “scan for emotion” part I started to feel that I wasn’t breathing properly and I was not relaxed at all. I think this was because I was trying too hard to find an emotion and I wasn’t able to sit there and “notice the absence of emotions”.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

Emotions are what you feel, not who you are.
