Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

While I initially thought that the mindfulness conversation was weird and uncomfortable after awhile I ended up enjoying it. When I was speaking I was able to tell the other person how much I appreciate them and they had to listen and take note of what I was saying. The other person enjoyed this as when someone says something nice to them they can usually brush it off/say something nice back and not take the compliment in. This time they did and appreciated what I was saying. I found the listening part difficult as I really wanted to speak to the other person. I now know that I probably jump into the conversation to early, thinking that I am contributing but really I am not let the other person finish what they are saying. This is something that I will need to work on in the future.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I will try to take one breath a day. As this is a very short amount of time each day, it will allow me to commit to it and hopefully sustain it.

Any other musings?

“Mindfulness is like exercise - You can only benefit from it with practice.”
