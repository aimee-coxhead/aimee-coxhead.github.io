Control

When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

I can be triggered when I find a problem difficult and I am unsure how to figure it out. I found it interesting when I read “Triggers are also very often connected to a perceived inadequacy about ourselves that is a source of pain to us, sort of like a raw nerve”. This really resonated with me as at the moment I am feeling like I don’t know/ understand enough. This can mean that if I don’t fully understand a problem or there are things in the problem that I haven’t learnt yet, it can causes a disproportionate emotional response and I can start to doubt myself and my ability.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

I found this exercise interesting. When a situation has passed it is easier for me to reflect on it in a nonjudgmental way and this was the case with this exercise. However, when a situation is happening in the present, I find it hard to take the emotion out of it. If I was to do the Siberian North Railroad practice during a present situation I think I would find it really difficult to be non judgmental.
